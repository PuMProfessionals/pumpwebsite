The Pathway Project

Medicine is undoubtedly one of the most intriguing and enticing career fields. However, students are often intimidated by the high standards of achievement and competitive nature.

Fear not– the PuMP team has compiled the important information to help you understand what the typical path to the medical field looks like! In this resource, we will explore the journey to becoming a health professional that everyone is familiar with – a physician (or doctor). This is an umbrella term for the many doctors that work directly with patients in diagnosing and treating disease, while also helping them maintain good well-being.

How to Use It!

Step 1

First, visit the pathway timeline to see a summary of each of the phases that a student goes through en route to becoming a medical professional.

Step 2

Then, proceed to read each core article for more details about the activities, opportunities and learning at each stage.

Step 3

Some sections have extra topics that can be explored by clicking links to extension pages.

PowerUp Mentor Program

Connect with students in undergraduate health science programs.

Nervous about university applications? Don’t worry, you’re in luck! PuMP Professionals and The STEM Fellowship have partnered to create PowerUp, a free mentorship program for those interested in health, life, and biological sciences! If you are in grade 11 or 12 and would benefit from speaking with a university student about scholarships, post-secondary applications, or university life, apply to be a PowerUp mentee! If you are a university student and would like to help younger peers, apply to be a mentor!
