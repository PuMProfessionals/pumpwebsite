MedWeb

MedWeb is an online webinar series that will expose high school students to various fields of study in the health sciences. These bi-weekly webinars running from October to December will alternate between featuring either a student or a professional from a certain specialty. Guest speakers will talk about their journey into medical school, talk about their specific field and interact with their audience by hosting a Q&A, playing games, and more!
