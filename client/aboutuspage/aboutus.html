About Us

A little more information about PuMP

Our Story

Prospective Medical Professionals, known as PuMP for short, is a federally registered non-profit organization on a mission to help Canadian high school and post-secondary students understand different career pathways in life and health sciences. Our goals are to:

  • Increase the transparency of the entry process into medical career paths
  • Make learning experiences in the healthcare industry more accessible for high school and post-secondary students
  • Promote equal opportunity for all youth, regardless of socioeconomic status, gender, religion or race

Our Discoveries

Founded in 2018 by a group of high school students, our team first sought out to tackle the lack of accessible learning opportunities for youth. We surveyed over 90 secondary school students in the Greater Toronto Area on their experience in health sciences, and here’s what we found:

80

out of 100 teens have considered a career in medicine

76

out of 100 of these teens are looking for learning opportunities

62

out of 100 of teenagers cite difficulties finding these opportunities

Our Focus

PuMP looks to make a difference by connecting students with learning opportunities, while also encouraging community involvement by providing resources, local events and conferences. By creating transparency between students and this ever-changing, competitive field, the organization looks to empower youth to continue exploring possible careers in medicine.
